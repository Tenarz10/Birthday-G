Remember how we first met? Everything felt so easy and nice back then.


I just want to wish you a really happy birthday 🥳


Thank you for everything you’ve done and for all the little things since we got to know each other.


Like you once said, you could order hugs from me like food 😂 Honestly, thank you for always coming to hug me too.


You also said so many things when we first started talking, and to be honest, I later realized a lot of them were actually true.


Sometimes I still wonder why you continued to stick around regardless.


And maybe that’s part of why some feelings are harder to quietly walk away from.


Life moves, people change, and sometimes distance settles into places that once felt familiar — yet somehow, certain people still manage to remain meaningful in ways that are difficult to properly explain.


I may not have been the choice you would have made eventually, but I’d be lying if I said your presence ever became ordinary to me.


So in light of that, I just want to say a really big thank you 🤩


I know we may not be as close as we used to be, but regardless of that, you’ve been truly wonderful.


Enjoy the rest of your day, and I genuinely hope this new year brings you happiness, peace, and beautiful moments.


Happy Birthday 🥳
Madam Globae ❤️
